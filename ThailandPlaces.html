Famous Places in Thailand

Bangkok

  • Capital city of Thailand
  • Famous for vibrant street life and temples
  • Home to the Grand Palace and Wat Arun

Chiang Mai

  • Known for ancient temples and night markets
  • Gateway to northern mountains
  • Popular for annual lantern festival

Phuket

  • Thailand's largest island
  • Famous for beautiful beaches and resorts
  • Great for snorkeling and nightlife

Khao Sok National Park

  • Stunning rainforests and limestone mountains
  • Home to Cheow Lan Lake
  • Wildlife includes elephants and gibbons

White Temple (Wat Rong Khun)

  • Located in Chiang Rai
  • Modern, ornate Buddhist temple
  • Famous for its striking white color and unique design

Ayutthaya

  • Historic city and UNESCO World Heritage Site
  • Known for ancient temple ruins and Buddha statues
  • Former capital of the Kingdom of Siam
